About Mya Khanz

Kyanz is a boutique perfume brand that blends classic floral notes with modern fragrance design. Each scent is crafted to tell a story – from gentle everyday elegance to bold, confident statements.

Our ladies collection captures the softness and grace of traditional blooms, while the men’s line reflects strength, character and modern masculinity.
